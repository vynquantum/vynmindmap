# Ho'oponopono Statement Generator <!-- vmm: {"note": "A framework for emotional awareness using Ho'oponopono prayers. Every emotion affects different areas of our life. Instead of asking 'What should I say?', ask 'Which area of my life is this emotion affecting?'"} -->

## Inner World <!-- vmm: {"note": "What's happening inside me?", "markers": ["priority-1"]} -->
- Self <!-- vmm: {"note": "How you relate to yourself (self-talk, self-worth, self-care, confidence, identity)."} -->
  - Ask Yourself <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - How am I treating myself?
    - What am I saying to myself?
    - Am I criticizing or accepting myself?
  - Example (Self Doubt) <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - I'm sorry for doubting my abilities.
    - Please forgive me for speaking harshly to myself.
    - Thank you for reminding me of my strengths.
    - I love myself exactly as I am.
- Thoughts <!-- vmm: {"note": "Mental patterns (overthinking, negative thinking, assumptions, stories, inner dialogue)."} -->
  - Ask Yourself <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - What thoughts keep repeating?
    - What story am I believing?
  - Example <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - I'm sorry for believing every fearful thought.
    - Please forgive me for replaying these stories.
    - Thank you for helping me notice my thoughts.
    - I love myself beyond my thoughts.
- Emotions <!-- vmm: {"note": "The feelings you are experiencing (fear, anger, sadness, guilt, shame, joy, anxiety, helplessness)."} -->
  - Ask Yourself <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - What emotion am I avoiding?
    - What emotion needs love?
  - Example <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - I'm sorry for holding fear inside me.
    - Please forgive me for resisting my emotions.
    - Thank you for helping me feel and heal.
    - I love every part of myself.
- Beliefs <!-- vmm: {"note": "Subconscious programs (usually start with 'I am...', 'Life is...', 'People are...', 'I can't...')."} -->
  - Ask Yourself <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - What belief is creating this feeling?
    - What do I believe about myself?
  - Example <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - I'm sorry for believing I am not enough.
    - Please forgive me for accepting this belief as truth.
    - Thank you for helping me create new beliefs.
    - I love myself beyond every limiting belief.

## Outer World <!-- vmm: {"note": "How I'm relating to life.", "markers": ["priority-2"]} -->
- Others <!-- vmm: {"note": "Your relationship with other people (comparison, expectations, approval, validation, judgments, relationships)."} -->
  - Ask Yourself <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - Who am I blaming?
    - Whose approval am I seeking?
    - Am I comparing myself?
  - Example <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - I'm sorry for comparing myself to others.
    - Please forgive me for seeking approval.
    - Thank you for the people helping me grow.
    - I love myself without needing validation.
- Actions <!-- vmm: {"note": "What you did or didn't do (avoidance, procrastination, people pleasing, not speaking up, overworking)."} -->
  - Ask Yourself <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - What action am I avoiding?
    - What action came from fear?
  - Example <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - I'm sorry for holding myself back.
    - Please forgive me for procrastinating because of fear.
    - Thank you for every step I have taken.
    - I love myself as I move forward.
- Control <!-- vmm: {"note": "Attachment, resistance, need for certainty (trying to control people, trying to control outcomes)."} -->
  - Ask Yourself <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - What am I trying to control?
    - What can't I accept?
  - Example <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - I'm sorry for trying to control everything.
    - Please forgive me for resisting uncertainty.
    - Thank you for teaching me trust.
    - I love myself as I surrender.

## Life Journey <!-- vmm: {"note": "My experience through time.", "markers": ["priority-3"]} -->
- Time <!-- vmm: {"note": "How your mind relates to Past, Present, and Future."} -->
  - Ask Yourself <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - Am I stuck in the past?
    - Am I afraid of the future?
    - Am I missing the present?
  - Example <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - I'm sorry for worrying about tomorrow.
    - Please forgive me for holding onto yesterday.
    - Thank you for this present moment.
    - I love myself through every season of life.
- Patterns <!-- vmm: {"note": "Repeated habits, cycles, emotional loops."} -->
  - Ask Yourself <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - What keeps happening again and again?
    - What lesson keeps repeating?
  - Example <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - I'm sorry for repeating this cycle.
    - Please forgive me for staying stuck.
    - Thank you for showing me this pattern.
    - I love myself as I create new habits.
- Growth <!-- vmm: {"note": "Learning, purpose, expansion, life lessons, future self."} -->
  - Ask Yourself <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - What is life teaching me?
    - How am I growing?
  - Example <!-- vmm: {"collapsed": true} -->
    - I'm sorry for doubting my journey.
    - Please forgive me for forgetting how far I've come.
    - Thank you for every lesson.
    - I love the person I am becoming.

## Core Guidelines <!-- vmm: {"markers": ["star"]} -->
- The Master Formula <!-- vmm: {"note": "When experiencing any emotion, ask:\n1. How am I treating myself? (Self)\n2. How am I relating to others? (Others)\n3. How am I relating to time? (Time)\n4. What am I thinking? (Thoughts)\n5. What am I feeling? (Emotions)\n6. What do I believe? (Beliefs)\n7. What am I doing? (Actions)\n8. What keeps repeating? (Patterns)\n9. What am I trying to control? (Control)\n10. What is life teaching me? (Growth)"} -->
- The Golden Rule <!-- vmm: {"note": "Don't try to write beautiful prayers. Write honest ones.\nYour goal is awareness, not perfection.\nThe most healing statement is usually the simplest and most truthful one."} -->
